The poutine is a classic Quebec dish composed of french fries, gravy and squishy cheese. This classic comfort food can easily be made at home with few ingredients and a bit of patience, the results are worth the effort!

Ingredients

  • Cornstarch
  • Flour
  • Water
  • Butter (unsalted)
  • Chicken & beef broth
  • Pepper
  • Potatoes
  • Cheddar cheese curds or mozzarella (as a substitute)
  1. Cut the potatoes into fries
  2. Put the cut potatoes in the frier
  3. Mix the broth in saucepan with Cornstarch and flour at medium heat until it thickens
  4. Adjust with pepper for flavour and water for consistency
  5. Put generous amount of cheese and sauce on your delicious fries
